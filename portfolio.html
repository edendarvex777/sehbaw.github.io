Cook With Me

Cook with Me is a designathon project I participated in. The collaboration of the local aging community and a startup platform, this designathon was created to allow technologists to either design or establish a technological solution for the case problem of how the older generation manuevers around their house.

DropIn

With a group of three other individuals for an information organization project, we were created a solution for finding a study place on a campus. Focusing on metadata, and communicating the information cohesively, we created a accessible design with a simple color pattern and a common UI to avoid information overload and to allow people's cognitive mechanisms to feel familiar.

Supporting Journalistic Transparency

With a group of three other individuals is a hackathon project I participated in with three other individuals. The process of the project began with using web scraping to obtain tools to code into three categories,
essentially creating metadata for each tool. Once the tools were categorized, we were then able to use Nielsen's ten usability heuristics to determine the usability of each tool.
